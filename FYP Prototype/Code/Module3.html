Do you understand what stunting mean?


It means children cannot grow to their full height and potential…
because of long‑term poor nutrition.

The effect is

serious

,

children may face weak health, poor learning, and limited futures.


But stunting is only one part of the story…


People who are stunted often face undernourishment… but undernourishment is not only about wasted food… It is also about poverty, inequality, conflict and climate change…

All of these limit access to enough healthy food. Wasted food makes the problem worse… Because while some food is thrown away, many people still go hungry.

This reminds us that hunger is not just about the amount of food produced. It is about whether food reaches every single individual…
